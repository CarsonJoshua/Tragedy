# 1-2 Playing Cards / Magic the Gathering Compatibility

## 1-2.2 Properties
* **1-2.2.1** Each playing card has a Color.  Playing cards of the Suit 'Spades' or 'Clubs' are black, and playing cards of the Suit 'Hearts' or 'Diamonds' are red.
